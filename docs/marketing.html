Explore, without a goal.

RelaxingPatterns is a gentle interactive experience designed to encourage brief moments of calm. Create soft patterns of light with simple taps and gestures while ambient sound and evolving visuals invite you to slow down, pause, and explore.

Touch, and something answers

Every tap sends out a slow bloom of light. Patterns overlap, fade, and settle at their own pace — there's no combo to chase and no score climbing in the corner. The interaction is the whole point.

Sound that listens back

Ambient audio shifts with what you do — softer when you slow down, fuller as patterns build. It's designed to sit in the background of a room, not demand headphones or attention.

A palette for how you feel

Multiple visual moods and color palettes mean the same gesture can feel different depending on what you're carrying that day. Switch moods the way you'd switch a lamp — no settings menu required.

Gentle, not gamified

Occasional reflective prompts invite attention without asking anything of you. There are no streaks, no notifications pulling you back, no goals to fall behind on. Open it for a minute or stay for an hour — both are the right way to use it.

Who it's for

Anyone who wants a short, unhurried break from a screen that's usually asking for more of their attention, not less. RelaxingPatterns doesn't try to replace stillness, therapy, or sleep — it's a small, quiet room you can step into between the louder parts of a day.
