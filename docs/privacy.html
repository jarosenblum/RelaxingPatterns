Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

What this policy covers

This policy describes how RelaxingPatterns ("the app") handles information when you use it. RelaxingPatterns does not require creating an account to use the app.

Information we collect

None. RelaxingPatterns does not collect analytics, usage data, crash reports, device identifiers, or any other personal information. Nothing about your use of the app is sent anywhere.

Third-party services

RelaxingPatterns does not integrate any third-party SDKs — no analytics providers, no crash reporters, no advertising networks, no backend services.

How information is used

Not applicable. Since nothing is collected, there is nothing to use, share, or sell.

Data storage and retention

Any preferences the app remembers (such as your last-used visual mood) stay on your device. Nothing is transmitted off your device or stored on any server.

Children's privacy

Because RelaxingPatterns does not collect any personal information from any user, this applies equally to users of all ages, including children.

Your choices

There's nothing to opt out of, since nothing is collected. Deleting the app removes any locally stored preferences along with it.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change. Material changes will be reflected here before taking effect.
