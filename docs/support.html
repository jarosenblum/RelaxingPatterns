Support

Answers to common questions, and how to reach us.

Frequently asked

How do I use RelaxingPatterns?

Open the app and tap or drag anywhere on screen. There's no tutorial to complete and no wrong way to interact — patterns respond to whatever pace feels right.

Does it need sound turned on?

No. The ambient audio adds to the experience but isn't required — everything works with sound off, including in Silent Mode.

Is my data private?

RelaxingPatterns doesn't require an account to use.

Can I use it for meditation or therapy?

RelaxingPatterns is designed for relaxation and enjoyment. See the note below on its intended use.
